Rolled Rings

TAILORED SOLUTIONS WITH TOP QUALITY & MATERIAL COST REDUCTION.

Our seamless rolled rings can be forged in configurations ranging from flat, rectangular-cross section, washer-like parts to tall, cylindrical shapes. They represent the absolute best option since they deliver higher strength and toughness versus the alternatives of castings, weldments or flame-cut plate.
