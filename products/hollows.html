Hollows

SUPERIOR STRENGTH WITH NO EXTRA TIME, COST OR MATERIAL WASTE.

The inside diameter of our hollows is forged into the part rather than bored from a bar, which makes them an excellent option that eliminates extra time, cost and material waste. Since we forge them as asingle component; we pass along several benefits to our customers by saving production time and increasing part strength.
