Facilities

At Frisa, we are forging the future.

Superalloys

Established in 2003 as a response to a growing demand, our Superalloys Plant specializes in seamless rolled rings for the Aerospace and Power Generation Industries. A few meters away from our Santa Catarina plant; it operates under 127, 713 sq. feet (11,865 sq. m) with state-of the-art technology and the most modern manufacturing systems. NADCAP and ISO 9100 & 14001 approved.

Santa Catarina

Our Santa Catarina facility, with 4 forging lines under 1,172,840 sq. feet ( 108,960 sq. m) of manufacturing space, has the capability and experience to offer high value forgings solutions in an ample variety of shapes, geometries and sizes.

Located in the heart of the Industrial strip of Monterrey Mexico, it ́s nearness to the US border (139 miles) places us in an ideal location for the shipping and transportation of our products to the rest of the world.

García

Our Garcia facility is located 6 miles (9.6 km) away from our others facilities. In 1,780,780 sq. feet(165,439 sq. m) of manufacturing space, it can satisfy your requirements for even the largest forgings. With the capability to produce complex shapes in various grades of Steel and Nickel base alloys, its state-of-the-art technology, and its location next to a principal railroad, our Garcia plant is an integral solution to your forging needs.

Open Die

Our Open Die facility is located in García, Nuevo Leon (6 miles away from our other facilities). With 90,000 sq. feet (8,000 sq. m) of manufacturing space, it was specifically designed to offer a wider range of open forging products in various grades of Steel and Carbon base alloys.

Equipped with state-of-the-art technology that enables us to produce larger, heavier and more complex shapes, Open Die plant represents a leap in our forging solutions portfolio.
