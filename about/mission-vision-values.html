About Mission / Vision / Values

Mission

To be the best option for each of our customers, offering high-value forging solutions, and aligning the company to meet the specific needs of each market segment.

Values

At Frisa, we promote an organizational culture based on our values:

  1. Customer Centricity and Service Attitude

  2. Quality Focus

  3. Honesty and Transparency

  4. Mutual Trust and Respect

  5. Humbleness and Ambition to Learn

  6. Accountability and Self-Management

  7. Teamwork and Interdependence

  8. Participative Decision-Making

  9. Commitment to Health, Safety and the Environment

  10. Recognition and Success Sharing

Vision

Our customers consider us to be

  • Reliable suppliers of goods and services with high added value

  • Their best option

  • Part of their competitive edge

  • Good for the long haul

Working toward the same mission and goal, our employees

  • Treat each other with trust and mutual respect

  • Are proud to work at Frisa

  • Consider it a place where there is always something new to learn and where they are fulfilled

  • Find economic, cultural, and social growth, for both themselves and their families

Knowing they can rely upon a long-term relationship, our suppliers

  • Are our partners in quality

  • Want to be our best supplier

  • Feel that we help them to improve

Our shareholders see us as a company that

  • Is highly profitable and with the potential for sustained growth

  • Can trust its employees

  • Is renowned for its highly ethical behavior

The community sees us as a company that

  • Has a sense of social responsibility

  • Is an example to be followed by other companies

  • Is renowned for its highly ethical behavior
