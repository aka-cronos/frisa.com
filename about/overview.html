About Mission / Vision / Values

We came into the industry over 45 years ago and we have come a long way since then. Through top-notch forging technology investments and nurtured expertise we’ve achieved continuous sustainable growth, positioning ourselves as one of the world’s leading forging providers.

But we know that we are not only in the forgings business, we are in the solutions business: creating value and delivering quality forgings that enable our customers confront the most demanding applications.

It is this conviction what has allowed us to grow and move forward: never stopping, always improving and preparing for a better future. A future we are forging today, overhauling standards, developing top quality steel grades, and offering comprehensive forging solutions through best-in-class technologies and practices for more flexibility, reliability and extraordinary customer value.
