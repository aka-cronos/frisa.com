Logistics

Worldwide door-to-door deliveries at competitive prices.

Services Heat treatment Machining Testing Product Development Logistics

We are committed to market needs, and that is how our shipment arrangements are made, taking into consideration the Customer Commitment Delivery Date

This lets us prioritize and swift shipments in order to reach and maximize the level of compliance.

Incoterms

  • Door to door Delivery (DAP)
  • Delivery at Port of Destination (CFR/CIF)
  • Delivery at Airport of Destination (FCA/CPT)
  • Delivery at the Border (FCA)
  • Delivery at Mexican Port (FOB)
  • Delivery to Plant (EXW)

Shipments are made as follows:

United States and Canada – ground transportation and/or railway South America, Europe and Asia – sea and/or air transportation.

We have the expertise and know-how to ship Regular and Oversized Rings by rail across the United States and Canada. We use different overseas modes of transportation such as Flat Racks and Break Bulk Cargo Shipments.
