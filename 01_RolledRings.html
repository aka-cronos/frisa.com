Rolled Rings

Tailored solutions with improved quality and material cost reduction.

The forging process for the seamless rolled rings circumferentially orients the grain flow, giving our pieces the mechanical properties they require to be more resistant to fatigue and impact, thus giving a better quality product capable of meeting your highest expectations.
Our seamless rolled rings have the ultimate in integrity and properties. They represent the absolute best option for any metal component with a ring-like geometry. Forged rings routinely deliver higher strength and toughness versus the alternatives of castings, weldments or flame-cut plate.

Seamless rolled rings can be produced in configurations ranging from flat, rectangular-cross section, washer-like parts to tall, cylindrical shapes.
